A playbook for Product Teams in the era of AI

The world of product development is rapidly changing due to the emergence of artificial intelligence (AI). AI has the potential to revolutionize the way products are created and managed, but it can be difficult for product teams to navigate this new landscape. In this post, I will outline a playbook for product teams looking to take advantage of AI in their product development process. First and foremost, product teams must understand the basics of AI. AI is a broad field of study that includes many different sub-disciplines, such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Each of these areas have their own unique set of tools, techniques, and best practices that should be understood before attempting to use AI in product development. Once the basics of AI are understood, product teams must identify which areas of their product development process can be improved through the use of AI. This could include areas such as user experience, product testing, feature engineering, and data analysis. Once these areas have been identified, product teams should begin to explore the different AI tools and techniques available to them. This could include open source frameworks such as TensorFlow and PyTorch, or commercial AI platforms such as AWS and Azure. Once the appropriate AI tools and techniques have been identified, product teams should begin to create a plan for incorporating AI into their product development process.
